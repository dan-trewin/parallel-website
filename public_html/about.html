This is me.. Dan Trewin!

Parallel Games is a small indie game development studio. And by small, I mean just me! I started parallel back in 2012 coinciding with the release of my first game - the app Block Ooo. Since then, I finished up my high school career and currently attend the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire.

If you're interested, you can check out my resume right here, or play around with some of my game jam games or side projects. They are all available to download and/or play on the games page.

Clearly, I don't work full time on game development, but I do love to program them in my spare time! I plan on releasing more quality and commercial games in the future. Please consider supporting me by downloading and purchasing my games. Thanks!

And if you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please send them to my support email - lavaflyer@live.com.
